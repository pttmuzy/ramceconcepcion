ABOUT ME

I am a Software Engineer and Web Developer from Philippines.

I was born and raised in the province of Rizal, Philippines -- a thriving region east of Metro Manila and just above the famous lake called Laguna de Bay. I am a degree holder of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduated from University of Rizal System, Binangonan Campus last April 2017. I spent most of my life in Morong, Rizal, and I still do, I work for a software company here as a software engineer.

I usually do the front-end side of the job, writing JavaScript for web applications of various nature. I also write JavaScript-based libraries for both fun and work, developing algorithms and UI components in order to make web development clean, professional and exciting. Sometimes I work on the back-end and occassionally design websites, such as this one, although this clearly isn't my best suit.
